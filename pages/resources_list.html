Rsources List
Here are some resources that you well need to survive in a natural disaster
You will need some items or resources to survive so we chose some to help you survive the natural disaster. First of all you will need a bagpack to carry things and a sleeping bag to sleep warmly. You will also need some some matches to start a fire and a lighter in case the matches get wet and become unusable. Next you will need some extra clothes to stay warm and to not catch a cold. You will also need some canned food to eat because if you dont eat you will have no energy. Nect you will need a bowl to cook in case you find something edible. You will also need a knife to chop down vegetables and to fight hostile animals. And lastly you will also need a rope to tie things if you need. You can also take things if you need other than the ones we came up with.
